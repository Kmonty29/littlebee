FAQ

Still have questions? Find the answer here!

  • How is my 401K setup?

    You can enroll at any time after your first paycheck! Click here for more information!

  • How do I change my tax deductions?

    Your federal tax deductions, address and direct deposit information can all be changed without any help from HR! Look for the pencil symbol in ADP to find out what you can edit.

  • How much parental leave do I get?

    Depending on your situation, you are eligible for a variety of different leave options, talk to your HR rep to find out which you can take.

  • I'm getting married. How do I add my partner to my benefits package?

    Congratulations! Your marriage is a qualifying life event. This allows you to alter your insurance coverage and plans outside of the normal enrollment period. !
